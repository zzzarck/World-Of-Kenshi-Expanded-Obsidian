Colonia
--
The easternmost continent of the planet, it is where the first group of the old colonizers arrived, dense forests dominate the continent and a solitary, giant mountain stands tall at the center of it. To the west, the massive mountain range forms a natural barrier separating this Colonia from Rom

Hiden
--
A continent that is directly connected to Colonia through land and separated by the western peaks mountain range. The mountains also split this continent into a northern region housing the secluded desert, and a southern region which is full of dangerous fauna and jungles.

Shattered Valley
--
Located in the West of the planet, a smaller continent that can be perceived as an archipelago, but is really many loosely connected islands by narrow bridges, the continent is key due to its high fertility soil and its importance as a giant trade checkpoint.

Twin
--
A continent divided by the "Bridge" Isthmus, its eastern side is full of plains and valleys, with a high mineral value under its land, while the western side is more full of life yet slightly colder
