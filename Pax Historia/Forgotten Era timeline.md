Pre-Colonization
--
The Vigil has sent construction teams to build hidden networks all across the planet, with 4 underground outposts on every continent, once they were built, they stationed surveillance teams and trained them to gather info about any future colonizers in the planet.

Year 0
--
This year marks the landing of both Concord and Acord into the planet, The Concord stablishing its first colony, going by the name of Primera Colonia, in the continent by the similar name "Colonia". The Acord sets their first colony in the continent of Hiden, directly on the Secluded Desert. This colony goes by the name of Tierra Nueva. 

Year 16
--
After 11 years since arrival, a new colonizer lands on the planet, The meritate sets their first colony on the western side of Twin, and starts stablishing their industry in the continent as fast as possible.

In the past years, Concord has expanded along the coast of Colonia with many outposts and developed Primera Colonia into a thriving capital, acting as the heart of the colony process.

Accord has also developed their capital in the Secluded desert, but instead of expanding on the same continent due to the isolation caused by the Western Peaks Mountain Range, they set began building outposts and docks all along the Shattered Valley continent.

Year 100
--

All factions has had time to develop their colonies, building new cities and outpost all along the continent, however tensions have started to grow due to the new borders created along the colonizers.

this same year the first war broke among the Accord and Concord colonizers, led by the outdated weapons and vehicles each faction was granted.

Year 102
--
The war between the Accord and Concord has been going for 2 years already and there is no clear winner in sight, however, this year also marks the year of the dissapearance of the planet.
