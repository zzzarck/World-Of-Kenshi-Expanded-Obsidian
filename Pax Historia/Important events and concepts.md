This section is more divided into events rather than a detailed timeline, as its much more abstracted.

The first partition
--
The first partition is the crisis the Concord faced after their overexpansion across the galaxy, the rising tension among sectors and horrible management form the Concord led to massive revolts and eventual Cutoff of almost half of the sectors the Concord owned, as a crisis management voted decided it was the only way to regain control over what they had.

The Galaxy Wide Wars
--
After the first partition, many of the sectors that were left to starve outside the sphere of influence of the Concord decided that expansion and unification of new empires was the only way to survive, half of the galaxy was quickly embroiled in massive wars that eventually became the reason the now hegemons exist.

The second partition
--
The downfall of the Concord continued after the first partition, as the overexpansion created a domino effect all over the nation, making it harder to recover as the year passed, not only that, but the Galaxy Wide Wars and the first partition itself created a massive influx of refugees coming back to the Concord, this led to a new problem that never stopped, overpopulation. The governors at The Concord eventually decided the only option was to cutoff all the frontier and unstable sectors from their sphere of influence, alongside impeding any access the refugees had into concord, leaving The concord with only 1/3 of the galaxy that they used to control.

Forgotten Planet Phenomena
--
The Forgotten Planet goes through a process where it dissapears and appears every few centuries, it gets sent to an abyss like place where the planet seems to be maintained artificially, with a fake sun and night cycle, seasons and climate. This is due to the planet not being natural, but built by an extremely ancient civilization, of which only remains lays here in the planet, what remains of them is a the individuals that control the artificial planets from its core, as it experiments on what happens with evolution of the people that attempt to colonize it.
